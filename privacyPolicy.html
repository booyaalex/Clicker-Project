Crystal Clicker Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes the information collected when using the website, and how it is used.


Last Updated: 18 September 2024



What Data is Collected

The website automatically uses cookies to save your progress in the game. The website also uses cookies to load your data from save files that you upload to the website.

This information is saved on the users device, and is not accessible to others.

These cookies are deleted after 30 days if the game has not played in that 30 day time period.


The website uses cookies to save your preferred settings.


How to Opt Out

The user is able to disable the saving mechanic on the website in the websites settings.
